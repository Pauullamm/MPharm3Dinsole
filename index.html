3D Scanning For Diabetic Foot Insoles

The use of customised insoles have been shown to prevent diabetic foot complications that arise in conjuction to diabetic peripheral neuropathy.



Our aim is to incorporate podiatric services in the context of community pharmacy through the use of 3D scanning technology to
